Facts About Earth

Layers Facts

-Earth has four layers the crust, mantle, outer core and inner core.

The crust is about up to 43.5 miles thick, the mantle is about 1801 miles and the outer core is 1367 miles and the last inner core is 950 miles. The crust is actually 2 billion years old. Crust is made out of rocks that are lighter then the rocks that make the mantle

Earths Creation Facts

-Earth formed over 4.6 billion years ago out of dust and gas around the young sun.

When the solar system settled into its current layout about 4.5 billion years ago, Earth formed when gravity pulled swirling gas and dust in to become the third planet from the Sun.

Current Earth Facts

-With a radius of 3,959 miles (150 million kilometers). -Earth is the biggest of the terrestrial plants and the fifth largest plant overall.
